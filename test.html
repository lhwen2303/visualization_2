Data Visualization Dashboard

A collection of interactive visualizations for dataset insights

Bar Chart

This bar chart represents the distribution of data over different categories. It allows you to compare various values visually.

Heatmap

The heatmap visualizes the intensity of values across two variables, with colors representing different magnitudes.

Stacked Area Chart

The stacked area chart displays cumulative totals over time, with different sections representing sub-categories.

Stacked Bar Chart

The stacked bar chart compares parts of a whole across different categories, showing how each category contributes to the total value.
